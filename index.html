About Us

iScientix targets to become an eco-system partner by providing end to end solutions to your digital transformation journey

  • History
  • Mission & Vision

iScientix was incubated in 2019 to become an eco-system partner for its customers for delivering accelerated discovery and Innovation through cost-effective service models. Our inception is powered by such unique opportunities in the post-pandemic battleground and our thirst to foster at highest customer satisfaction through innovative consulting.

Covid Crisis has necessitated businesses to be more agile and responsive; thereby accelerating the pace of transformation across the industry in all sectors. It has accelerated digital transformation journeys; reinforced the importance of applying exponential technologies to produce more efficient, effective, and flexible processes.

Such a Digital transformation doesn't happen in Isolation. Organisations expect this transformation to flow from open engagement with customers and eco-system partners. Leveraging such partners to find solutions to large-scale problems require a completely different set of tools and mindset.

Mission

Mission is to become a successful eco-system partner in Luxembourg by achieving highest customer satisfaction through tailor made digital transformation solutions.

Vision

Vision is to benefit from post pandemic industrial scenario by offering an ecosystem model driven by accelerated discovery and innovation along with cost-effective technology consulting.
